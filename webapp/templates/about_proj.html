Voice Conversion


Voice conversion (VC) is a technique to modify non/paralinguistic information of speech while preserving linguistic information. This technique can be applied to various tasks such as speaker-identity modification for text-to-speech systems speaking assistance, emotion/expressiveness conversion and pronunciation conversion.


We had implemented two methods for voice conversion:

  1. Gaussian Mixture Models

    2. A parallel voice conversion method

  2. Cyclegan-VC2

    3. A non-parallel voice conversion method
